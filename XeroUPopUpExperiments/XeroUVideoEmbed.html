Using Tracked Inventory

As we saw in Getting Started with Tracked Inventory, Tracked Inventory delivers simple inventory management allowing you to be more efficient with real-time access to the on-hand quantities and value of your inventory. Items can be easily added to quotes, invoices, POs and bills helping you to improve sales and meet demand. In this video we’ll dive into using Tracked Inventory on sales and purchases, tracking on hand quantities & profitability and viewing patterns with the Inventory Dashboard and Reporting.
